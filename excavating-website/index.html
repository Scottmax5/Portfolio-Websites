About us

With years of industry experience, our team of skilled professionals can provide the most cost effective and streamlined execution to handle any project big or small. We specialize in a range of services including site preparation, foundations (residential or commercial), land clearing and demolition, trucking, aggregate sales and more.

At Ainsworth Excavating, we pride ourselves on delivering high quality driven earthworks and excavation solutions. We provide exceptional customer service and building lasting relationships with our clients.

Our commitment to safety, environmental responsibility, and quality craftmanship has earned us a reputation as one of the most reliable excavation and earthworks companies in the Okanagan Valley.

Whether you need excavation services for a small residential project, or a large commercial development, Ainsworth Excavating is the company to call. Contact us today to learn more about our services, rates, and how we can help bring your next project to life!
