Heritage Classic

Heritage Classic watch, large model, Manufacture mechanical movement with automatic winding, caliber 1847 MC. Steel case, PVD bezel, steel crown set with a faceted blue synthetic spinel, gray striated dial, steel sword-shaped hands with luminescent material also on the hour markers, sapphire crystal. Steel strap with "SmartLink" adjustment system. Second strap in blue rubber, with interchangeable steel deployant buckle. Both straps are fitted with the "QuickSwitch" interchangeable system. Case width: 39.8 mm, thickness: 9.38 mm. Water-resistant up to 10 bar.
